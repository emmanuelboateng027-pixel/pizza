Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our service

How often is the bed status updated?

Bed availability is updated in real-time by hospital staff. The dashboard auto-refreshes every 30 seconds to show the latest information.

Can I book a bed through this system?

No, this platform only provides information about bed availability. To book or reserve a bed, you need to contact the hospital directly using the phone numbers provided.

What should I do in a medical emergency?

For life-threatening emergencies, call 193 immediately for the National Ambulance Service. Don't wait to check bed availability - emergency services will coordinate with hospitals.

How can my hospital join this system?

Hospital administrators can contact us via email at info@nobedsyndrome.gov.gh or call +233 302 123 456 to discuss integration with the platform.

Is this service free to use?

Yes, the No Bed Syndrome platform is completely free for public use. We aim to improve healthcare transparency and accessibility across Ghana.

About No Bed Syndrome
